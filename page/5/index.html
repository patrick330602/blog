About

Currently working as a software developer in Canonical mainly developing Ubuntu for WSL, and persuing MSc on Computer Science in CUHK.

Graduated from HKUST as BEng on Computer Science, Main developer of WSL Utilities, and UWP Developer as WE. Studio. Previously help developed Pengwin at Whitewater Foundry, and founder of (no longer active) Windows Phone community WPerfect.
